Pawxa Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary:
Pawxa is designed to respect your privacy. Most data stays on your device and is used only to provide app features.

1. Information We Handle

Pawxa may store information that you enter, such as:

  • Pet details (name, breed, age, weight)
  • Health check-ins and activity logs
  • Nutrition and behavior data

This information is primarily stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide health insights and analytics
  • To improve app performance and features
  • To enable reminders and tracking features

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your data with third parties except when required for app functionality or legal compliance.

4. Permissions

  • Internet: Required for app functionality and updates
  • Notifications: Used for reminders

5. Data Security

Your data is protected using standard Android security mechanisms. We take reasonable steps to protect information stored within the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Pawxa is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Control

  • You can clear app data anytime from settings
  • Uninstalling the app removes stored data

8. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
